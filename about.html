Our Mission

Our mission is to equip students with the highest level Christian education so that they will be equipped for college and the world.






School District covers over 50,000 residents and 10,000 students. We have 2 high schools, 4 middle schools and 12 elementary schools.

Our History

School District started in 1960 and educates children based on Abeka curriculum and state standards. Our district is #1 in graduation rates and we continue to strive yearly. School District is an EEO employer and only employees full-time educators so that our mission will be completed.

Demographics

Our student body represents a rich cultural identity that identifies with the image of God. Average class sizes are between 15-20 students. School District strives to not extend past 20 to keep a personal approach to learning.

Curriculum

The curriculum is consistent with state standards and with Abeka guildelines. We chose Abeka because of their rich-accelerated content that students can grow and develop with. This is adapted to individual student needs. Students are able to score higher on tests on the average, than those who are not taught in this curriculum.

Abeka Curriculum Provides the following:

  • Lesson plans that break down lessons so that they are manageable and easy to understand
  • Biblical content teaching solid principles
  • Textbooks and visual aids that are age appropriate
  • Digital curriculum for technology standards

After 8th grade, students attend our state top-rated high school which prepares them for college. The High School's curriculum is designed to provide collegiate-level studies to each student so that they can accel academically. For more information about our high schools, please call our administration office.
