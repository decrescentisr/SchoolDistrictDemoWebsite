Mission Statement

Mission Statement

Our mission is to equip students with the highest level Christian education so that they will be equipped for college and the world.


Our Beliefs

  • The curriculum we use lines up with solid biblical principles each student will apply in the world.
  • Active physical activity of at least 30mins per day increases attention span and learning comprehension in the classroom.
  • Each student has their own learning style and trait which makes them unique.
  • Learning is life-long.
  • Our schools are safe and inviting to any student.
  • We set goals so that students can achieve and develop into the skills required.
  • All students deserve a quality education.

Our Goals

  • The School District will implement a biblical curriculum to equip each student for the Matthew 28:18-20 commission.
  • The School District will empower students to demonstrate, personal, social, and educational growth.
  • Each student will grow based on benchmarks and curriculum provided. This will be measured by quarterly assessments.


School District is a full service school district website focusing on providing information and solutions to parents, educators and students.
