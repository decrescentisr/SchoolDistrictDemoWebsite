OUR VISION



Our vision is to prepare students for their future educational goals while impacting each student with a high-quality education.



STRATEGIC PLAN



Our plan is designed to be a roadmap for the School District. It is a common foundation of expectations and goals to prepare students and staff for success. Our goals are:


  • Kindergarten Readiness
  • Individual IEP (Individualized Education Plan) focused on career interest
  • High Graduation Rates
  • High Completion and Attendance Rates
