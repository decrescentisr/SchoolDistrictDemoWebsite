Our Students

Our students are a vital part of the School District. Anywhere from learning to playing, students enjoy their time within our school system.

Our Students


About Our Student Philosophy

We have a set philosophy that each student will be branded into.

Each student must have fun learning while maintaining biblical principles so that they can fulfill the Matthew 28:18-20 command.

Demographics

Our student body represents a rich cultural identity that identifies with the image of God. Average class sizes are between 15-20 students. School District strives to not extend past 20 to keep a personal approach to learning.

Physical Activity

The curriculum is designed to include at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily.

Research states:

  • Physical activity increases cognitive understanding and comprehension
  • Increases attention span and alertness
  • Increases metabolism and prevents obesity
  • Allows for positive thinking due to the release of endorphins

Students will not be disappointed attending the School District schools. We offer a plethora of extracurricular activities.

Activites include:

  • Football (middle and high school)
  • Soccor (elementary through high school)
  • Softball (middle and high school)
  • Basketball (elementary through high school)
